Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our remodeling process

How long does a typical remodeling project take?

Most projects range from 8-12 weeks depending on scope and complexity. Kitchen and bathroom remodels typically take 6-8 weeks, while whole-house renovations can take 12-16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.

Do you help with design decisions?

Absolutely! Our design team works closely with you to create a space that reflects your style and meets your functional needs. We provide 3D renderings, material samples, and expert guidance throughout the design process.

How do you handle unexpected issues during construction?

We conduct thorough pre-construction assessments to minimize surprises. If unexpected issues arise, we immediately communicate the situation, provide solutions, and discuss any impact on timeline or budget before proceeding.

What's included in your project warranty?

We offer a comprehensive warranty covering workmanship for 2 years and materials according to manufacturer warranties. All warranty details are provided in writing before project commencement.

How do you ensure the project stays on budget?

We provide detailed, transparent quotes and maintain open communication about costs. Any change orders are documented and approved before work begins. Our project management system tracks expenses in real-time.

Can I stay in my home during the renovation?

For most projects, yes. We work to minimize disruption and maintain clean, safe work areas. For major whole-house renovations, we can discuss temporary living arrangements and phase the work to maintain livability.
