Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical remodeling project take?

The timeline varies depending on the scope of the project. A kitchen remodel typically takes 4-8 weeks, while a bathroom renovation may take 2-4 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations to discuss your project, assess your space, and provide preliminary recommendations. This helps us understand your vision and determine if we're a good fit for your project.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. ABC Company is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry both liability insurance and workers' compensation to protect our clients and our team throughout the project.

Do you work with a fixed budget?

We work with budgets of all sizes. During our consultation, we'll discuss your budget and provide options that align with your financial parameters while delivering quality results.

Can you help with design ideas?

Yes, our design team works closely with clients to develop concepts that match their style preferences and functional needs. We can work from your ideas or develop complete design solutions.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a 5-year craftsmanship warranty on all our work, in addition to manufacturer warranties on materials. We stand behind our work and are committed to your long-term satisfaction.
